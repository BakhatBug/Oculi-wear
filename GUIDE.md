# Sleep Cycle Optimizer - User Guide

Welcome to the **Sleep Cycle Optimizer**! This extension is designed to help you maintain a healthier sleep routine by managing your screen time, protecting your eyes, and reducing digital distractions as your bedtime approaches.

---

## 🌙 Core Features

### 1. Adaptive Dimmer
- **Manual Mode**: Toggle the **Dim** button in the popup at any time to darken your screen. This is perfect for late-night work or reducing eye strain in dark environments.
- **Automatic Schedule**: The extension automatically starts dimming your screen as you approach your bedtime.
    - **Phase 1 (Warning)**: Slight dimming to remind you that bedtime is near.
    - **Phase 2 (Winding Down)**: Increased dimming and a reminder banner.
    - **Phase 3 (Bedtime)**: Maximum dimming intensity with a persistent reminder to stop using the device.

### 👁️ Eye Comfort Mode
- Applies a warm amber filter to all pages to reduce blue light exposure. This helps your body produce melatonin naturally, making it easier to fall asleep.
- Adjustable intensity via the Settings page.

### 🎯 Focus Mode
- **Website Blocker**: Automatically blocks "Sleep Disruptors" (Social Media, Streaming, News) during your bedtime phases.
- **Customizable**: You can define your own list of disruptive sites in the Settings.

### 😴 Snooze & ✅ Done
- **Snooze**: Postpones the bedtime warnings for 10-30 minutes if you need a little more time to finish a task.
- **Done for Tonight**: Turns off all dimming and warnings until the next day. Use this when you are actually finished with your screen for the night.

---

## 📊 Insights & Tools

### 🏆 Sleep Score
- A daily score from 0 to 100 based on your "Digital Hygiene."
- Points are deducted if you spend time on disruptive websites during your bedtime window. Aim for 100!

### 📈 Weekly Trends & Categories
- **Weekly Score**: Track your consistency over the last 7 days.
- **Category Breakdown**: See where your time goes (Streaming vs. Social vs. News).

### 🧘 4-7-8 Breathing Exercise
- A guided breathing tool to help relax your nervous system before bed.
- Follow the rhythm: Inhale (4s) → Hold (7s) → Exhale (8s).

### 🧹 Close Disruptors
- Instantly closes all open tabs that are classified as "Sleep Disruptors" to help you disconnect quickly.

---

## ⚙️ Configuration
Access the **Settings** page from the popup to customize:
- Your target **Bedtime**.
- **Warning Window** duration (how many minutes before bedtime the dimmer starts).
- **Intensity** levels for both Dimmer and Eye Comfort.
- **Custom Domains** you want to block or track.
